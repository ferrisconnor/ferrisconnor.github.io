Welcome to Stress Less

Stress is something that everyone experiences at some point. It can be caused by school, work, family, or other challenges in life. While some stress is normal, too much can have a negative effect on both your mental and physical health.

Everyone experiences stress differently, and the things that cause stress can vary from person to person. Learning to recognise the signs of stress and understanding how it affects your body and mind is the first step towards managing it in a healthy way.

This website has been created to help you better understand stress and discover simple, practical ways to manage it. Whether you're feeling stressed yourself or just want to learn more about the topic, you'll find useful information and ideas throughout the site.
